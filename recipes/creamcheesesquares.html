Cream Cheese Squares

Nutrition Facts

213 calories per serving (24); protein 2.8g; carbohydrates 18.4g; fat 14.2g; cholesterol 20.5mg; sodium 245.8mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8oz) cans refrigerated crescent roll dough
  • 2 (8oz) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup margarine, melted
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit and grease a 9x13in pan
  2. Press one can of dough into the bottom of the pan
  3. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, sugar and vanilla until smooth. Spread over the dough layer
  4. Unroll the second can of dough and lay it on top of the cream cheese layer DO NOT PRESS DOWN
  5. Pour the melted margarine, 1/4 cup sugar and cinnamon over the pan
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes
